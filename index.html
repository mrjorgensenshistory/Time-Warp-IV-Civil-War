The Civil War involved millions of people and hundreds of thousands died. Many of those involved remain nameless - lost to history but some people stand out. Today you will play as those people through a series of levels to experience life around the time of the Civil War. As you click on the boxes the story will advance. Use the information available to you to help make the best decisions and ensure you are remembered by history. The best choice is always whatever really happened in history. Each character in the game made a unique mark on history. Your goal is to make the right decisions so they are remembered in history. If you are forgotten, you've lost.
